About Me

Hi! I’m Keyla, the founder of Sorelle & Co., a brand built with love and a passion for healthy living. What started as a small kitchen experiment turned into a journey of creating meals that are not only nourishing, but also full of flavor and joy.

Through Sorelle & Co., I hope to inspire others to embrace clean eating as a lifestyle — one that’s sustainable, beautiful, and full of energy. Whether it's a vibrant salad or a refreshing detox drink, every product is made with care and intention.
